BIO

// PLAYER PROFILE

GAME DESIGNER, SOFTWARE ENGINEER, AND WORLD-BUILDER

I'm Kay Desist, a developer focused on building interactive systems that feel clear, responsive, and memorable. My work sits between game design and software engineering: prototyping mechanics, shaping player flow, and turning ambitious ideas into playable, testable experiences.

I like projects with a strong mood and a practical backbone. Whether I am building a Unity prototype, a web tool, or an AI-assisted workflow, I care about readable code, usable interfaces, and design choices that support the person on the other side of the screen.

Current quest: create polished, atmospheric software that keeps the wonder of games while still feeling professional, maintainable, and easy to use.
